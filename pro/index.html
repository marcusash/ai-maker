Open any terminal (or just double-click install.bat)

2
Download and run the Red Pill installer
irm https://github.com/marcusash/ai-maker/releases/download/v3.0.0/install-red.ps1 | iex

> BACKUP AND RESTORE
Your ai-workspace folder is a private GitHub repo. Your vault, persona, and workspace files are committed and pushed automatically during setup. To restore on a new machine:
1
Run the Red Pill installer on your new machine
Same command as above. The installer detects your existing ai-workspace repo on GitHub and restores everything automatically.
2
Authenticate with GitHub
Sign in when prompted. The installer clones your private repo, installs all 22 skills, and puts everything back where it belongs.
3
Open the App and pick up where you left off
Your vault, persona, and workspace files are exactly as you left them. Your agents remember everything.
