# ai-maker-brainstorming

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Pill:** blue
**Category:** creativity

## Purpose
Generate structured brainstorming output: divergent ideas, evaluation criteria,
and a ranked shortlist with rationale.

## Usage
Describe the problem or question you want to explore. AI Maker will generate
a structured set of options and evaluate them.

## Example
> Brainstorm three approaches to reduce meeting fatigue in our team.
