How it works
Up and running in three steps.

No technical setup required. You download a ZIP, run the installer, and your AI assistant takes it from there.

1

Download the package

Click the Download button to get the AI Maker ZIP file. Save it anywhere on your machine, like your Desktop or Downloads folder.

2

Double-click setup.bat

Extract the ZIP, then double-click setup.bat in the extracted folder. It handles everything: renames the scripts, requests admin access, and launches the full installer automatically.

Need help? The Install Guide walks through every step with screenshots.

3

Launch and build your profile

Open AI Maker from your desktop shortcut. It will interview you about your role and build a personal AI assistant trained specifically to how you work.
